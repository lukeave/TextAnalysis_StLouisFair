CATLINS GIVE DINNER \ FOR.MISS ROOSEVELT.  Mr. and Mrs. Daniel Catlin and Miss Catlin gave a dinner to sixteen guests last night in honor of their visitors, Miss Alice Roosevelt and Miss Alice Warder.  Dinner was served in the large dining- reom. which was trimmed with a profu- sion of pink roses. A round table was used. As the Calin party had arranged to remain at the Fair yesterday until quite Inte, the dinner hour was set for half after $ o'clock, and the evening hours after din- ner were spent at the Catlin home.
